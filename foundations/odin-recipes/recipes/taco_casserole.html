Best Taco Casserole

This taco casserole with ground beef and tortilla chips is easy to make and very tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • ½ cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.

Credit : Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Step 2

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes.

Step 3

Stir in salsa, reduce heat, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Stir in beans and cook until heated through.

Credit : Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Step 4

Spread crushed tortilla chips over the bottom of the baking dish. Spoon beef mixture on top. Spread sour cream over beef, then sprinkle olives, green onions, and tomatoes on top. Cover with Cheddar cheese.

Credit : Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Step 5

Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.

Credit : Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Step 6

Serve and enjoy!
