Italian Sausage Tortellini

Tons of flavor in this tortellini with Italian sausage! Your family will love this!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 (8 ounce) package baby portobello mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup dry white wine, divided
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced, divided
  • 10 ounces mild Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package three-cheese tortellini
  • 14 ½ ounces Alfredo basil sauce
  • 1 (5 ounce) package baby spinach leaves

Directions

Step 1

Heat butter in a large, deep skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir mushrooms, 1/2 cup white wine, and 1 clove minced garlic until liquid has reduced and mushrooms have softened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and place in a bowl.

Step 2

Heat the same skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir Italian sausage until browned and crumbly, about 5 to 7 minutes. Drain excess grease and return sausage to the skillet.

Step 3

Return cooked mushrooms to the skillet. Add tortellini, Alfredo basil sauce, remaining white wine, and garlic. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 4

Add spinach, cover, and cook until spinach is wilted, about 3 to 4 minutes. Mix well to combine.

Step 5

Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your meal!
